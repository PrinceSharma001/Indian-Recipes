Paneer Kathi Roll

This Paneer Kathi Roll is a delicious vegetarian Indian street food wrap. In this 30-minute recipe, paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices is cooked with vegetables, then stuffed inside a crisp paratha, for a roll that will please vegetarians and meat-eaters alike!

Ingredients

  • 40g Tomatoes
  • 60g Peas
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 0.5 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2g Chilli
  • 0.25 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 3g Coriander
  • 1 x Roti (regular)
  • 80g Paneer
  • 20g Bell Pepper
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Crumble the paneer
  2. Heat the oil in sauce pan over meduim heat
  3. Add green peas and chopped chillo stir for about one minute.
  4. Add Tomatoes, salt and masala, mix well.
  5. Cover the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes till tomatoes and peas become soft.
  6. Add chopped Bell pepper
  7. Add paneer stir and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add chopped coriander leaves and mix.
  8. Roll paneer bahji in 2-3 rotis to make kathi roll
